# Sales Data Visualization Project

## Description
This project analyzes sales data for different products and demonstrates various data visualization techniques using **Seaborn** and **Matplotlib**.
It includes visualizations of product categories, total sales, monthly trends, and price distribution.

### Visualizations Included:
- **Count Plot** – Distribution of product categories
- **Bar Plot** – Total sales by product category
- **Line Plot** – Monthly total sales trend
- **Box Plot** – Price distribution by product category

### Insights
- Easily identify the most popular product categories.
- Observe monthly sales trends and peaks.
- Understand price variation across categories.
